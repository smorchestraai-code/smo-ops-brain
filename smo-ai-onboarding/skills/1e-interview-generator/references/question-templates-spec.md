# Interview Question Templates

10 dimension templates + tools inventory + conflict resolution.

## Naming
`_templates/Interview-Questions/<dimension>.template.md`
`_templates/Interview-Questions/_tools-inventory.template.md`
`_templates/Interview-Questions/_conflict-resolution.template.md`

## Per-Dimension Template Structure

5-10 questions per sub-field. Stage A picks 1-3 based on which sub-fields scored low.

Example for founder-os:
- Sub-field background: "Walk me through 3 most relevant career roles and lessons each taught about <industry>."
- Sub-field vision: "What does <brand> look like end of 2027? Specifically: revenue, customers, team, geography."
- Sub-field operating_style: "What time of day are you sharpest? When are you slow?"
- Sub-field principles: "What's a deal you turned down in the last year and why?"

## 10 Dimensions

founder-os, resources, goals-and-constraints, mena-context, company-profile, offer, icp-and-segments, positioning-and-voice, competitive-context, current-priority-project.

## Tools Inventory (always 12)

CRM, email infra, email marketing, LinkedIn, ads, scraping, AI agents, video, design, scheduling, payments, analytics.
Per tool: vendor, account status, current usage, willing to expand.

## Conflict Resolution

Per 1b coherence pair <8.0: "We see <signal A> in <source A> and <signal B> in <source B>. These conflict because <reason>. Which is correct, or both true in different contexts?"

## Language Variants

Each template in en, ar, bilingual.
